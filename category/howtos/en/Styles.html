Where to get styles

  • Tenr.de - Tenner has many very good styles, and makes them all the time. Check his site often
  • box-look.org - Also styles for other boxes
  • Customize.org - Haven't checked this one yet, looks quite promising
  • Klowner - Klowner has some 6 quite nice styles

Install styles

In order to install them, download and extract the tar archive in your ~/.fluxbox/styles directory.
Then you'll find them in your Fluxbox menu under the styles entry.

Make a style

Tenner has some very good info on making styles on his page at tenr.de
